E-learning Deputyship

E-learning is the process of learning that occurs using the internet and learning technologies to deliver educational and instructional materials and allow communication between faculty and students, students and students and students and learning materials. The Royal Commission of Jubail Colleges and Institutes Sector is integrating technology into the sectors educational pedagogical strategies.

Our Objective

To integrate technology into the pedagogical and educational processes of the RCJ Colleges and Institutes Sector.

Our Vision

To support the Sectors educational institutions and their members’ by meeting their technological needs and supporting faculty in the development of quality online courses and programs to improve student learning outcomes and productivity.

Our Mission

To provide the highest quality online learning opportunities by supporting faculty and staff through:effective instructional design, proper integration of instructiona technologies, professional interactive digital media development, and the deployment and support of new education and communication technologies.

E-learning Deputyship Services

  • eLearning technical support.
  • Technology related workshops and e-training.
  • Develop eLearning materials for faculty and both academic and administrative departments.
  • Provide analytical data on Blackboard usability and utilization.
  • Develop eLearning materials for special programs.
  • Provide Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs) for the community.
  • Portable Computer Labs.
